Request to Delete Your Feminine Flow Account

We understand that you may wish to delete your Feminine Flow account. We respect your privacy and are here to help.

To request account deletion:
  • Email us at: tkplushcollections@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Include your registered email address in the message body.

What happens next?

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Verify your request within 3 business days.
  • Delete your data (journal entries, to-do activity, saved content, and personal info stored in Firebase).
  • Send you confirmation when the process is complete.

Thank you for using Feminine Flow. We hope to serve you again in the future.
