Privacy Policy – Feminine Flow

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for login and subscription purposes)
  • Journal entries, to-do activity, and gratitude reflections
  • Device data (device ID, app version, usage activity)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core app features like journals and voice notes
  • Offer personalized content (e.g., Weekly Wisdom, Daily Facts)
  • Track your app activity to generate progress summaries
  • Manage premium access through payment verification
  • Communicate updates and provide user support

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on:

  • Firebase for authentication, Firestore data, and file storage
  • Paystack for subscription payments and verification

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and practices. We do not store credit card details.

4. Data Security

We store data securely using Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. Access is protected using authentication, and sensitive data is never shared or sold.

5. User Rights

  • View or delete your journal and account data
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Revoke your consent for data use by deleting your account

To make a request, please email us at: tkplushcollections@gmail.com

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such information has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” above and notify you within the app or via email.
